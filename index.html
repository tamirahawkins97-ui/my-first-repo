Bumblebee also known as Karen Beecher, first appeared in Teen Titans #45 in December 1976, though she did not adopt the Bumblebee identity until Teen Titans #48 in 1977.She is known for being one of DC Comics’ first African-American female superheroes and for her powers, which originally came from a scientific, solar-powered suit that allowed her to shrink to insect-like size, fly with bee-like wings, and fire sonic force blasts or electrical “stings”
